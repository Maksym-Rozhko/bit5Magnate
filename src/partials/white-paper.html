BIT5 NFT GAME

The game is based on the logic of the MONOPOLY board game. The difference is that our users will not be constantly online, but will fight for virtual assets non-stop.

For a large number of participants, our playing field will consist of 100x100x100x100 cards. Of these, 90% are company cards, and 10% are bonus cards or dummies

User's task: to collect shares of the company of one of the lines of the square field

Our game is divided into 4 stages:

  • Deposit money into the game from the wallet
  • Buying “bones” for a step in the form of NFT
  • Buying shares of a company in the form of NFT
  • Reset quarterly
  • Step 1. The user must deposit in the form of USDT / BUSD tokens to the wallet inside the game (as it happens for example on Binance or any other CEX)
  • Step 2. The number of NFT “Bones” is limited to 10 million pieces. The maximum quantity for one wallet is 1000 pieces*. The usage limit is 1 time per 5 minutes. The user buys an NFT “Dice” for X amount, which randomly generates a move. As a result of a virtual move, the user gets a card (like how you threw the dice in a real game, looked like a chip and hit some kind of card).
    • if it is a company card without an owner -> the user can buy it for USD
    • if it is a company card with an owner -> the owner of the card receives an annuity (tax) payment from the user's deposit
    • if it is a chance card -> the user gets X number of bit5 tokens or the right to roll the dice again
    • if it's a dummy card -> nothing happens, users lost the turn
    As a result of the move, NFT “Bones” burns out.
  • Step 3. If at step 2 the user gets a card that does not have an owner, he can buy it for USD and keep it in his wallet until the end of the round.
    In the in-game marketplace, the user can buy a company from another player at a price set by the owner. There is a 10% commission on every resale.
  • Step 4. After using 10 million steps in the form of NFT dice or after 30 days, a new field is launched in which 80% of new companies, and 20% from previous stages.

Victory: the user who collects shares of one side of the field receives $1,000,000, and if no one has collected a line before the end of the steps or 30 days, then the user with the maximum number of sets (cards of the same color) receives $100,000, and $10,000 is distributed in equal parts among the other cardholders.
